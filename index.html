Hi, my name is Jason Jagosh. I love to create & entertain. I currently work as a freelance writer, graphic artist, audio-visual producer, and concert promoter in Tampa, FL. I enjoy making people happy, good music, maintaining a balanced life, understanding the universe around us, and cats.

Since I have a passion to make cool stuff and a knack for computers, it was only a matter of time before I got into coding. With advances in modern computing technology, I have gained a burning desire to learn what makes the Internet click how people from all over the world can connect in seemingly infinite ways.

Where I'm From

I was born in and grew up in Bradenton, FL. I spent a lot of my time outside on the beach, in a swimming pool, or climbing every tree in my backyard. When it would get too hot outside I would come in and play my Nintendo 64 or make cool art on the computer.

I graduated from the University of South Florida in 2013 with degrees in marketing and management. I want take what I've learned from business school and focus on online marketing.
